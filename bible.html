Faith and Reflection

These verses have shaped how I see purpose, discipline, and peace. Each one reminds me to stay grounded in what truly matters — not just what’s visible, but what’s eternal.

Proverbs 3:5-6

“Trust in the Lord with all your heart and lean not on your own understanding; in all your ways submit to Him, and He will make your paths straight.”

This verse keeps me centered when life feels unpredictable — a reminder that surrender can be strength.

Isaiah 40:31

“But those who hope in the Lord will renew their strength. They will soar on wings like eagles; they will run and not grow weary, they will walk and not be faint.”

Faith doesn’t remove exhaustion; it transforms it into endurance.

John 1:1-5

“In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God.”

The start of everything — light breaking through chaos. That’s where creation and creativity meet.

“Let the Word live through your work, not just your words.”
