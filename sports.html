My Sports Journey

Growing up in Aberdeen, sports have always been a major part of my life. I played basketball and soccer throughout my youth, constantly striving to improve and compete at higher levels. My senior year of high school was particularly transformative as I transferred from Aberdeen Central to Aberdeen Roncalli and experienced a significant growth spurt, going from 5'9" to 6'4".

Basketball has always been my dream, and I feel incredibly blessed to continue pursuing it at the collegiate level here at Trinity Bible College. The dedication, hard work, and perseverance required have shaped not just my athletic career, but also my character and discipline off the court.
